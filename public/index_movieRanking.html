The Best Movies According to Rafa

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Everything, Everywhere, All at Once

This is my favorite movie of all times, because I think it is so deep and full of layers. You can watch it multiple times and still be amazed by it.

Parasite

A great Korean movie that won its first Oscar for Korea in 2022. Talks about class disparities in the world.

Get Out!

This one impressed for its terror. Talking about racism in a horror movie was really groundbreaking. Amazing casting.
